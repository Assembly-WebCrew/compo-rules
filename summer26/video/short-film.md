# Short Film

Refer to the [General rules](../general.md) with the following additions:

- The film must be original and unreleased.
- If you use Creative Commons or Public Domain material, state so in your entry form and cite the source.
- AI-generated content is not allowed in this compo. This means the entry's own content: the footage, animation, graphics, music, and any code must be human-made. Tools you use to make the entry (such as synths, trackers, packers, editors, compilers, etc.) are fine even if AI was used to develop those tools - using them does not make your entry AI-generated.
- Fan art using copyrighted characters or works is not allowed.
- The production must be submitted in a video format supported by VLC for Windows. Recommended: 1920x1080 at 50 or 60 fps, x264 in an MP4 container with a high bit rate - other VLC-playable formats are fine too, but this one looks best on the big screen.
- The film may not last longer than **5 minutes**.
- Computer-generated pieces of art (e.g. animations, machinima, etc.) are preferred over plain home video types of entries.
- Videos of real-time generated graphics on odd devices belong in the Real Wild demo competition.

Remote entries are welcome.
